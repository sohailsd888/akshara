Our Faculty

We believe in zeal of faculty, We Choose the best qualified, passionate and dedicated teachers to suit the classes. Highly professional, discipline specific educators chosen from KERALA occupy 80% of our teachers. It help us to actualize 100% English language skills as well as over all developments in student community.
Along with subject teachers we have special teachers to promote extra curricular activities like sports,music, arts,dance, yoga and martial arts.
Well trained Pre primary teachers are another distinct feature of our school.one teacher for one class makes students feel a homely atmosphere in school. Audio visual classes And gradual escalating mode of teaching enable the students to follow and reach our Target. A complete syllabus oriented education is provided to pre primary sections what make them learn and speak English language right from nursery standard. There are non teaching female staff to attend to every needs of small students. They assist them in natural needs, lunch periods and break times.
