About Us

Welcome to Akshara High School
Established in 1994, Akshara High School is dedicated to excellence in education. Our mission is to provide quality learning experiences that inspire and empower students to achieve their fullest potential. With a commitment to fostering a supportive environment and promoting lifelong learning, we invite you to be a part of our vibrant community. Join us as we embark on a journey of knowledge, growth, and success together. At Akshara, we believe in nurturing not just academic success, but also character development and leadership skills, preparing students for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow.

As Chairman, I believe in fostering an environment where discipline is not just a rule, but a guiding principle. Our commitment to discipline is not about restriction, but about empowerment, enabling students to achieve their full potential. Through a culture of respect and accountability, we aim to cultivate responsible individuals who are prepared to navigate the challenges of tomorrow. Join us in our journey of nurturing minds and shaping futures through the power of discipline. Together, let's create a community where every student thrives and succeeds. Thank you for being a part of our vision."

-Mr. P.S.Ashokan , Chairman, Akshara E/M High School, Manthani

As Director of akshara We are committed to fostering creativity and high achievement in every student. Our curriculum is designed to inspire innovative thinking and prepare students for success in government offices, corporations, and beyond. At AKSHARA, we encourage students to explore their passions, think critically, and embrace challenges as opportunities for growth. Our dedicated faculty are here to guide and support students on their journey to excellence. Through a combination of rigorous academics and hands-on experiences, we equip students with the skills and mindset needed to thrive in today's dynamic world. Join us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow, where creativity meets achievement.

-Mrs.Bindu Ashok, Director, Akshara E/M High School, Manthani

As we embark on this journey together, we are excited to share our vision for the future of our school. Through collaboration, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to our core values, we aim to continue providing unparalleled educational experiences that empower our students to thrive in an ever-changing world.

-Dr.Swathi Ashok Puthiyedan (PhD) , Executive Director, Akshara E/M High School, Manthani

As member of the advisory board, we are committed to nurturing our students to reach the highest positions and fostering their creativity. Our goal is not only to guide them towards success in their professional endeavors but also to help them thrive personally. Together, let's empower our students to excel in all aspects of their lives."

-Mr.Akshay Puthiyedan (MBA T&H) , Advisory Board, Akshara E/M High School, Manthani

Our Campus

Manthani is a municipal town in Manthani mandal in Peddapalli district of the Indian state of Telangana. Akshara English Medium High School functions in a three storied building situating on the banks of "vagu" beside the bridge. It is a fantastic campus owing 20 independent rooms and corridors. Every rooms are well ventilated and blessed with sunlight abundantly. Public ground and open gym close to the building is another attraction of our school.
